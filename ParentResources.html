HOW TO FIND THE JUST RIGHT BOOK


1.) STEP 1--Choose a book that you think you will enjoy.
2.) STEP 2--Read the second page.
3.) STEP 3--Hold up a finger for each word that you are not sure of or do not know.
4.) STEP 4--If there are 5 or more words that you did NOT know, you should choose an easier book!
