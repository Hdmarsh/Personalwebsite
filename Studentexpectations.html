Mrs. Marsh's Expectations


On this page you will find things you should DO, things you should NOT DO, and some words of inspiration for succeeding in my class!








THINGS STUDENTS MUST DO TO BE SUCCESSFUL IN MY CLASS




Bring required supplies to class.
Be confident and attentive.
Be respectful of others.
Respect others' property--including mine and the school's!





THINGS STUDENTS MUST NOT DO TO BE SUCCESSFUL IN MY CLASS


Do NOT interrupt others!
DO NOT move about without permission!
Do NOT have your phone or other electronics out in class!
Do NOT be afraid to ask for help!
